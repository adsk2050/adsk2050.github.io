Favorite project

IITG Mechanical department website

Me and my friend and bachelor thesis project partner Dhiraj built this awesome website for the Mechanical department of IIT Guwahati, my alma mater.

It started as a summer project in May 2019. I mainly worked on the backend and Dhiraj made sure it looked nice. It started with making something similar to a wireframe model and showing it to our project advisor, Prof. Nelson Muthu. We started working on it and after multiple iterations, we ended up with this.

It was a really challenging project for me personally because it exposed me to creating a piece of software from scratch. I got to understand Wagtail and Django in depth, and also many other small aspects that I would never have come across if I was interning in a huge company, where there is a team for everything. It was also a test of patience for me because I was very rigid at the begining, about what I made is the best. But slowly I started realizing what building for customer means. I had an awesome mentor, Raghuram Krishnaswamy who showed the way throughout. This project is the closest to my heart because I owned its development, fixed the bugs and devoted a good amount of time to it. It has also helped me gain skills which are helpful even now.

Before we graduated, we found an awesome guy, Sudesh and handed it over to him. He has made more optimizations and created Academics section from scratch.

Technologies used:

  • Wagtail
  • Bootstrap
  • CSS
  • HTML
  • Azure cloud VM
  • Shell scripting
  • Apache http server
